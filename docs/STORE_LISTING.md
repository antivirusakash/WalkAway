## Description

**Walk away. It locks. Come back. Keep working.**

WalkAway watches the Bluetooth signal between your Mac and your Apple Watch. When you step away, your Mac locks itself within seconds. When you sit back down, it's right where you left it. You never expose your screen to the room again — and you never wait through an idle timer while you're sitting right there.

**Why it's different**
- **Presence, not timers.** Idle-lock timers either lock while you're reading or leave your Mac open for minutes after you've gone. WalkAway locks on *distance*, not inactivity.
- **Reliable departures.** Tuned so leaving always locks — even when macOS reports your watch's signal intermittently. If your Mac can't confirm you're nearby, it locks.
- **Stays out of your way.** Won't lock mid-sentence: while you're actively typing or moving the mouse, it holds off until you're truly gone.
- **Set the distance.** Choose how far is "away" — default 6 meters, adjustable from 2 to 20.

**How it works**
1. Pick your Apple Watch from the menu bar.
2. Set your lock distance and grace period.
3. Walk away. Your Mac locks itself.

**Privacy first**
WalkAway runs entirely on your Mac. No account, no cloud, no tracking, no data leaves your device. It only reads the Bluetooth signal strength of the watch you choose.

**Built for menu bar living**
Lives quietly in your menu bar. Launches at login. Shows live signal and status at a glance.
