# WalkAway — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

Hosted version: _to be published_

WalkAway is a macOS menu-bar app that locks your Mac when your paired Apple
Watch moves away. The short version: **WalkAway collects no personal data, has
no account or login, and transmits nothing off your Mac.**

## Data we collect

**None.** WalkAway does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit any
personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no
tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

## What the app accesses on your device

- **Bluetooth signal:** WalkAway reads the Bluetooth signal strength (RSSI) of
  the Apple Watch you select to estimate proximity and decide when to lock the
  screen. Used only on your Mac, in real time; never recorded or transmitted.
- **Your settings:** Preferences (selected device, lock distance, timers) are
  stored locally via macOS user defaults and never leave your device.
- **Diagnostic logs:** Operational logs (lock decisions, on-device performance)
  are written to Apple's local unified logging on your Mac. They stay on your
  device and are not accessible to us.

## Network use

WalkAway makes **no network connections**. It works fully offline and never
contacts any server, including ours.

## Permissions

WalkAway requests **Bluetooth** permission solely to detect the proximity of
your chosen Apple Watch. It does not access contacts, location, camera,
microphone, photos, or files.

## Children's privacy

WalkAway collects data from no one, including children, and is not directed at
children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version is posted at the hosted URL above
